Hours & Location

Stop by Growl! at 2995 N. High St. for a pint, glass of wine, or classic cocktail! Located in the heart of Clintonville, Growl! is easy to get to by car, bus, bike, or even on foot. We’re open the following hours:

  1. Monday–Thursday 4:00pm–11:00pm
  2. Friday–Saturday 12:00pm–1:00am
  3. Sunday Closed

Happy hour every Monday through Thursday from 3-7pm! Enjoy $2 off draughts.

Parking is available behind the building on W. Tulane Rd. Check the signs for designated Growl! spots. Additional parking is available along N. High St. and in the surrounding neighborhood.

Avoid parking issues by taking public transit, biking, or walking! There’s a COTA stop right outside the door and the Olentangy Greenway Trail is less than half a mile away.
