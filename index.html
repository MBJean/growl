Hours & Location

Stop by GROWL! at 2995 N. High St. for a pint, glass of wine, or classic cocktail! Located in the heart of Clintonville, GROWL! is easy to get to by car, bus, bike, or even on foot. We’re open:

  1. Monday–Thursday: 4:00pm–11:00pm
  2. Friday–Saturday: 12:00pm–1:00am
  3. Sunday: Closed

Why GROWL?

“Good beer,” “cozy,” “ a great neighborhood bar,” “friendly,” “chill,” and “positive” are how our customers describe GROWL! So if you’re looking for a welcoming and relaxed atmosphere , then GROWL! might be the place for you!

GROWL! prides itself on being a communal space. Come by and borrow a book from our little library, check out our art collections and local good for sale, or play a game with friends—borrow one from our collection or bring your own.
